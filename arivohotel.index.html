Help Centre

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Arivo Hotel Transfer.

Booking
How do I book a transfer? +

Simply click "Book a Ride" in the navigation, fill in your flight and hotel details, and submit the form. You'll receive a confirmation email within 15 minutes with your driver's details and a booking reference.

How far in advance should I book? +

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to guarantee availability. However, we do accept last-minute bookings subject to driver availability — contact us via WhatsApp for urgent requests.

Can I modify or cancel my booking? +

Yes. Modifications can be made up to 4 hours before your pickup time at no charge. Cancellations made 12+ hours in advance are fully refunded. Please contact us via email or WhatsApp to make changes.

At the Airport
Where will my driver meet me? +

Your driver will be waiting at the arrivals hall holding a name board with your name clearly displayed. The exact meeting point will be included in your booking confirmation email.

What if my flight is delayed? +

No worries — we track all flights in real time. If your flight is delayed, your driver will automatically adjust their arrival time. There are no extra charges for flight delays. You don't need to call us.

How much luggage can I bring? +

Standard bookings include up to 2 large suitcases and 2 carry-on bags per person. If you have extra luggage or oversized items (surfboards, strollers, etc.), please mention it in the special requests field when booking.

Pricing & Payment
How is pricing calculated? +

Pricing is based on the airport, destination hotel, and vehicle type. All prices are fixed — no surge pricing, no hidden fees. You'll receive a full price quote in your booking confirmation before any payment is required.

When and how do I pay? +

Payment is due upon confirmation of your booking. We accept online bank transfer, credit/debit card, and cash payment to the driver. Corporate accounts can arrange monthly invoicing.

Do you offer corporate rates? +

Yes! We offer discounted corporate rates for businesses with regular transfer needs. Contact us via the Contact page or email us at hello@arivohotel.com to discuss a corporate account package.
